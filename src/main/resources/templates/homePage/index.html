Welcome to the organizer app!


Organizer is a web application for project management that is modeled on Jira software. The organizer allows, among other things:

  • Adding, modifying and managing projects
  • Carrying out the project independently or in a group
  • Creating new tasks and assigning them to other users
  • Editing tasks
  • Progress tracking
